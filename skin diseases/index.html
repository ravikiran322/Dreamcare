Skin types

Find a routine that fits your skin’s personality.

Whether your skin feels tight, shiny, or somewhere in between, Dreamcare helps you build a simple, sustainable routine.

  • Dry skin: replenish with ceramides and lipids.
  • Oily skin: balance with light textures and non-comedogenic actives.
  • Sensitive skin: prioritise fragrance-free formulas.

How to identify your type

  1. Cleanse, then wait 30 minutes with no products.
  2. Observe shine, tightness, or irritation.
  3. Note how products feel during the day.
  4. Use this information to fine-tune your regimen.

Skincare tips & guides

Daily habits that protect your skin.

Small, consistent habits can radically improve skin resilience. Build a custom routine across sun care, nutrition, and hygiene.

Apply broad-spectrum sunscreen, SPF 30 or higher, reapplying every two hours or after swimming or sweating.

Start with three steps: cleanse, moisturise, protect. Layer in targeted treatments slowly.

Learn how humectants, emollients, and occlusives work together to keep skin comfortable.

When to seek help

  • Sudden changes in mole shape or color.
  • Painful or rapidly spreading rashes.
  • Recurring infections or non-healing wounds.
  • Symptoms impacting sleep or daily activities.

Key ingredients

What’s inside your routine matters.

Hyaluronic acid

Hydration

A powerful humectant that attracts and binds water to help keep skin feeling plump and comfortable.

Ceramides

Barrier support

Lipids that help reinforce the skin barrier so it can retain moisture and defend against irritants.

Niacinamide

Tone & calm

A form of vitamin B3 that supports a more even look and helps ease visible redness.

Glycerin

Everyday comfort

A gentle, widely used humectant that draws water into the outermost layers of skin.
